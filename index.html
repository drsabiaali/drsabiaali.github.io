My Services

As a Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT), I offer a range of specialized services to help you recover from injuries, manage chronic conditions, and enhance your overall physical function. Below are the specific services I provide:

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Specialized therapy to help you recover from orthopedic surgeries and injuries such as fractures, sprains, strains, and arthritis. My approach focuses on restoring strength, flexibility, and function to affected areas.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Rehabilitation for patients with neurological conditions like stroke, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and spinal cord injuries. The goal is to help regain independence, mobility, and reduce the impact of neurological impairments.

Pain Management

I provide non-invasive pain management solutions to address chronic pain conditions such as back pain, neck pain, joint pain, and fibromyalgia. My approach includes manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, and lifestyle recommendations.

Sports Rehabilitation

Custom rehabilitation programs designed for athletes recovering from sports-related injuries. The goal is to return you to your sport safely and quickly, while improving overall performance and preventing future injuries.

Geriatric Physical Therapy

Focused rehabilitation for older adults dealing with age-related conditions, including osteoporosis, arthritis, and balance disorders. The aim is to improve strength, mobility, and overall quality of life.

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

After surgical procedures such as hip or knee replacements, I assist with rehabilitation to restore range of motion, strength, and function, ensuring a smooth transition back to daily activities.

Balance and Fall Prevention

Specialized exercises to improve balance, coordination, and prevent falls, especially for older adults or those recovering from injury or surgery. Fall prevention techniques are incorporated into daily routines to reduce the risk of injury.

Pelvic Health Therapy

Targeted therapy for pelvic floor disorders, such as incontinence, pelvic pain, and post-pregnancy recovery. Therapy aims to strengthen pelvic muscles and restore normal function for improved health and comfort.
